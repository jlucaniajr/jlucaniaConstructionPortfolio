About Me

I am an aspiring construction project professional currently transitioning from a background in computer science, bringing with me a strong foundation in data analysis, stakeholder coordination, and process optimization. Through my experience working with large datasets and building tools to support business decision-making, I've developed a detail-oriented and analytical approach to solving complex problems—one that I am now excited to apply in a more tangible, project-driven environment.

During my time in technology-focused roles, I collaborated closely with cross-functional teams and senior stakeholders to define requirements, streamline workflows, and deliver solutions that improved efficiency and clarity across organizations. Whether developing dashboards used by leadership or designing systems to track and optimize processes, I've consistently focused on creating tools and structures that help teams operate more effectively.

My transition into construction is driven by a desire to work on projects with visible, real-world impact. I am particularly drawn to the coordination and execution aspects of construction—where planning, communication, and problem-solving come together to bring complex projects to life.

I bring a mindset rooted in adaptability, continuous learning, and accountability. Combining my technical background with a growing understanding of construction practices, I aim to contribute as a project engineer or coordinator, supporting teams in delivering projects efficiently, safely, and to a high standard.
